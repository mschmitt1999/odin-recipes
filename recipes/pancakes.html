Pancakes the german way

Description

Super fast traditional german dish. You can enjoy it sweet with sugar and cinnoman or you can eat it satly with chees.

Ingredients

  • Eier
  • Milch
  • Mehl
  • Zucker

Steps to Make It

  1. Crack two eggs into a bowl
  2. Give some milk into it
  3. Give it some flour
  4. brat es :)
