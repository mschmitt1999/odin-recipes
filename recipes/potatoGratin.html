potato gratin

Description

It's just a wonderful dish to eat and to make.

Ingredients

  • potato
  • ½ l milk
  • 3 gloves of garlic
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps To Make It

  1. Peel the potatos
  2. Cut them to little slices
  3. Cut the garlic
  4. Pour some oil into a baking pan
  5. Add the garlic
  6. Place the slices like the skin of a snake
