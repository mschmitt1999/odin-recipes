Spaghetti with tuna sauce

Description

If you want to eat fast, but don't want to eat fastfood. This dish is prepared in 10 minutes and it's super tasty

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of tuna
  • 200ml cream
  • ½ onion
  • 1 glove of garlic
  • 3 peperonis
  • salt
  • pepper
  • spaghetti

Steps To Make It

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. When the water is boiling add the spaghetti.
  3. Cut onions the garlic and the peperonis.
  4. Fry them in a pan
  5. Add the tuna to it
  6. Add the cream
  7. When the spaghettis are ready the sauce also is ready
